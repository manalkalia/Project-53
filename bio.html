Biography Of Usain Bolt



Usain St. Leo Bolt OJ CD OLY is a retired Jamaican sprinter, widely considered to be the greatest sprinter of all time. He is the world record holder in the 100 metres, 200 metres, and 4 × 100 metres relay.
